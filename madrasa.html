Children's Madrasa

We are an Islamic Supplementary Evening School which offers Islamic Studies, Qur’an Recitation and Qur’an Memorisation classes according to the true teachings of the Qur’an and prophetic ways of the Final Messenger – Prophet Muhammad.

The Madrasah’s aims are to fulfil the obligation of seeking knowledge in a caring and secure Islamic environment, to endorse excellent manners and nurture future leaders of our community.

By providing a well-structured curriculum, and by endorsing excellent manners through our Behaviour Policy and Rewarding System, we aim to encourage in all children the love of learning of the deen as well as the respect and care for one another.

We highly recognise the great importance of the partnership between home and the Madrasah in producing well-rounded confident Muslims; we firmly believe that collectively we can offer a unique education to our children.

The health, safety and welfare of our students, families and community are at the forefront of the decisions we make as an institute. In light of the current health emergency and rapid spread of COVID-19, we have taken the decision to suspend the children's classes until further notice.

During this period, we expect students to study online with us using the Zoom online platform we have set up.

Please contact the Imam or Apa for further information. Lessons will start from Monday, 30th March onwards.
