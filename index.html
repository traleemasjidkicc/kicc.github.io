Ramadan News

Assalaamu Alaykum,

With the profound changes that the current Pandemic has brought upon with it, we would like to keep you informed about the services and activities at our Masjid.

Jumuah & daily Congregational Salaah

Friday prayers and other congregational prayers remain suspended until further notice due to the ban on public gatherings by the government. We will revise this decision in light of the Government’s announcement after the lockdown. We are taking these measures in line with the Government guidelines and advice so kindly we would appreciate your full co-operation.

Ramadhan 1441AH/ 2020

Ramadhan this year is expected to start on Friday 24th April. First Taraweeh Salaah could be performed on Thursday 23rd April. A precise announcement shall be made closer to this date. Please keep updated through our website.

Taraweeh

Everyone is requested to perform their Taraweeh Salaah at home throughout Ramadhan. This is a great opportunity to pray as a family where the mature male will lead the others in Salaah and the women of the house can join.

Children’s Madrasah Classes

The Madrasah shall continue online via the Zoom app. For further information or queries please contact the Imam or Apa.

Womens weekly Class

The weekly class for local women will continue via phone etc. Please contact Apa for further information.

Activities in Ramadhan

To avoid disruption to our normal routine in the Masjid, we have set up a Live Audio platform for all programmes. The following programmes will be Live on Masjid Mixlr streaming channel.

  1. Every Wednesday after Asar (approx 8pm) – Hadeeth Lesson
  2. Every Friday after Asar (approx 8pm) – Fiqh Masaail Lesson
  3. Every Sunday after Asar (approx 8pm) – Bayaan/Speech

GP/Health Clinic Service

Doctors from our community have volunteered to give their time to the community. For medical consultation or help related to Covid-19 or any other issues call Manor Medical Centre on 0667122599.

Donations for our Masjid projects and services

Please continue to donate generously throughout Ramadhan as it is a month of generosity and spending in the path of Allah. To pay your annual Sadaqatul Fitr of €7 per person or give your Zakaat or any other donations then please contact Dr Altaf Memon on 0866038982 or the Imam on 0862440556.

Let us turn to Allah Ta’ala with Dua and Istighfaar in these testing times. May Allah Ta’ala grant everyone protection, ease & comfort. Aameen.

Tralee Masjid Management Committee

About KICC

Welcome to the official website of Kerry Islamic Cultural Centre, Tralee.

Our objective is to promote religion in accordance with the tenets and doctrines of the Islamic faith while adhering to Islamic values and we are committed to the general welfare of all our members so that they may contribute to the well being of the community at large.

We promote education and teachings of the Islamic faith for the benefit of the Muslim community in Tralee, Co Kerry.
