New Masjid Construction

Asalaamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullah wa Barakatuhu.

All praise is due to Allah (سبحانہ وتعالی) who is the most kind and most merciful. The Kerry Islamic Cultural Centre (Tralee Mosque) member community are planning to start the construction of a 2-storey mosque structure. The Management Committee would like to thank the community and the donors for their continued support.

"He who built a mosque for Allah (سبحانہ وتعالی), the Exalted, Allah (سبحانہ وتعالی) would build for him a house in Paradise." - Al Hadith [Sahih Muslim Book 4 No. 1084 & 1085]
